Strategic Services

Catalyst Legal Nurse Consulting provides litigation-relevant expertise across a range of clinical domains. Each service is tailored to support attorneys with clear, defensible insights grounded in decades of healthcare leadership.

  • Medical Record Review: Comprehensive analysis of EMRs, facility documentation, and clinical timelines.
  • Wound Care Litigation Support: Expert evaluation of wound progression, treatment protocols, and standards of care.
  • Long-Term Care Case Consulting: Insight into nursing home operations, staffing, compliance, and resident outcomes.
  • Compliance & Regulatory Review: Identification of violations, gaps, and policy failures tied to federal and state standards.
  • Expert Witness Preparation: Strategic guidance for deposition, testimony, and cross-examination readiness.
