Privacy Policy

Catalyst Legal Nurse Consulting is committed to protecting the confidentiality of all client communications and case materials. We adhere to HIPAA standards and legal best practices to ensure that sensitive information is handled securely and appropriately.

Information We Collect

  • Attorney contact details and firm information
  • Case-related documents and medical records
  • Optional intake form submissions

How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate with legal teams regarding case progress
  • To maintain secure records for internal review and compliance

Data Security

All uploaded files and communications are stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. We do not share, sell, or disclose client information to third parties without explicit consent.
