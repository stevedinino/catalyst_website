Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Certified Legal Nurse Consultant (CLNC)?

A CLNC is a registered nurse with specialized training in legal consulting. Janet uses her clinical expertise to analyze medical records, identify standards of care, and support attorneys in litigation involving healthcare issues.

How does Janet support attorneys in litigation?

Janet provides case screenings, merit reviews, medical chronologies, expert opinions, and strategic insight into long-term care, hospice, wound management, and compliance-related cases.

What types of cases benefit from nurse expert review?

Cases involving nursing homes, hospice care, wound care, medication errors, staffing issues, and regulatory compliance are ideal for legal nurse input.

What credentials does Janet hold?

Janet is a Board-Certified Adult-Gerontology Nurse Practitioner, Certified Legal Nurse Consultant, Licensed Nursing Home Administrator, Wound Care Certified Specialist, and Certified in Health Care Compliance.

How is Janet’s clinical experience different from a typical nurse?

Janet has served as Chief Clinical Officer, Director of Operations, and Regional President in senior care organizations. Her leadership and compliance background give her a unique lens on healthcare litigation.

What types of medical records should I send?

Send all relevant documentation including progress notes, MARs, care plans, incident reports, wound assessments, and any regulatory correspondence. Janet will guide you if additional records are needed.

How does Janet handle HIPAA compliance?

All records are handled securely and confidentially. Janet follows strict HIPAA protocols and offers encrypted intake options via the Upload page.

What’s the turnaround time for case reviews?

Typical turnaround is 5–7 business days depending on case complexity. Rush reviews may be available upon request.

Can Janet testify as an expert witness?

Yes. Janet is qualified to testify in cases involving long-term care, hospice, wound care, and nursing home operations. She also supports attorneys with deposition prep and expert report drafting.

How do I initiate a case review?

Visit the Contact page to reach Janet directly, or use the Upload page to submit records securely.

Where can I learn more about Janet’s services?

Visit the Services page to explore detailed offerings including screenings, chronologies, and expert consulting.
